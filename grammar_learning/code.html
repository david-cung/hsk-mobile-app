Grammar: Question Particle 'ma'

HSK 1 Grammar
lightbulb

Key Concept

Adding 'ma' at the end of a statement turns it into a yes/no question. It is one of the simplest and most common ways to ask questions in Mandarin Chinese.

Formula

[Statement] + 吗?

Tone Tip

"ma" is always neutral tone (5th tone).

Examples

你好吗? Nǐ hǎo ma? (How are you?)
他是学生吗? Tā shì xuéshēng ma? (Is he a student?)
warning

Common Mistakes

Don't use 'ma' with question words like 'shei' (who), 'shenme' (what), or 'nali' (where). The question word already makes it a question!
